PartnershipHousing + Workforce
Connecting Homelessness Services to Workforce Development
Contra Costa County

A landmark initiative catalyzing a guaranteed income pilot and $4.25 million in county funding. Loren contributed lived experience consultation to bridge the gap between homeless services and workforce pathways — designing structures that administrative data alone would never have revealed.

CoCo Go BIG guaranteed income pilot launched · $4.25M in county funding secured
Dalbert DesignHousing + Planning
East Contra Costa Mobility LABs Initiative
Contra Costa County

Led as program manager, this community-designed initiative developed mobility solutions for underserved East Contra Costa communities. Lived experience guidance and participatory design helped catalyze significant county investment.

Community-designed solutions adopted at county level · Pilot funding secured
PartnershipCivic Fellowship
Lived Experience Leadership Institute (LELI)
Marathon & Phillips 66 · Contra Costa County

A fellowship training young adults ages 18–26 from five unincorporated Contra Costa communities in civic intelligence research, AI-directed analysis, and policy engagement — our methodology, codified as a training program.

Cohort 1 launching August 2026 · leli.design ↗
CivicMakersEngagement + Housing
Co-creating Accessible Affordable Housing with Seniors, Unhoused Communities & People with Disabilities
Bay Area Housing Authority

A co-design process centering multiple vulnerable populations to shape accessible affordable housing policy — moving from listening sessions to actionable design recommendations with real community ownership.

Policy recommendations adopted · Accessible design requirements strengthened
CivicMakersWorkforce + Engagement
Uplifting the Voices and Needs of Women Entering the Trades
California Workforce Development

A service design engagement surfacing structural barriers women face entering trade careers. Participatory research, co-design sessions, and actionable recommendations for workforce development programs statewide.

Recommendations integrated into statewide workforce program design
CivicMakersInnovation Lab
CA Health & Human Services Innovation Lab
California Health & Human Services Agency

Multi-year engagement supporting the CA HHS Innovation Lab with co-design and facilitation for agency staff at all levels. Built internal capacity for collaborative problem-solving across California's largest health and human services system.

"Exceeds expectations" — Director, Office of Innovation, CA HHS
CivicMakersStrategic Planning
Community Advisory Board Strategic Plan
City of Santa Rosa

An iterative strategic planning process charting an actionable roadmap for Santa Rosa's Community Advisory Board — with robust engagement from both City staff and community to build capacity and cultivate long-term ownership.

Contract extended for implementation coaching · Workplans mapped to strategy
CivicMakersTraining + HCD

A multi-year training partnership building HCD capacity across City of San José departments — creating space for staff to experience collaborative problem solving and embedding a design culture from the inside out.

Long-term partnership · HCD practice embedded across multiple departments
