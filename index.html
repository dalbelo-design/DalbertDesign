Strategic & Implementation Planning

Planning built from the inside out.

Dalbert Design partners with county departments, government agencies, and their consultants to strengthen homelessness and housing response systems — surfacing the gaps that administrative data misses and helping institutions build solutions that work for the people they are designed to serve.

Who We Are

Dalbert Design is a lived experience–led organization founded by Loren Dalbert. Our work integrates the direct knowledge of what it takes to navigate homelessness and housing systems with the analytical capacity to translate that knowledge into institutional action — co-authoring the systems that govern people's lives alongside the departments that administer them.

What We Do

Strategic and implementation planning services

01

Strategic plan development

We contribute lived experience analysis to county and regional homelessness strategic planning processes — identifying trust and dignity gaps, centering the perspectives of people with direct experience of homelessness, and ensuring that plan language reflects the real conditions people navigate rather than an idealized version of the system.

02

Implementation planning and oversight support

We help implementation committees and county departments translate strategic plans into operational practice — designing the structures, protocols, and feedback mechanisms that keep implementation grounded in community reality as it unfolds.

03

Lived experience integration

We design and support the structures that make lived experience participation meaningful rather than performative — including advisor selection, preparation and compensation frameworks, pre-implementation review processes, and ongoing mechanisms for community intelligence to reach decision-makers.

04

Gap analysis and service system diagnostics

We identify where referral pathways break down, where resources are nominal rather than real, and where system design is producing predictable failures that data systems do not capture — informing both immediate course corrections and longer-term system redesign.

Our Approach

Lived experience tells you what is wrong and points out the gaps. Living expertise helps you build it right. Both belong in the room where implementation decisions are made.

Why Dalbert Design

A perspective most planning tables don't have

Dalbert Design brings the direct knowledge of what it takes to navigate these systems — combined with the analytical capacity to translate that knowledge into institutional action. We work alongside county departments and their partners, not as outside critics, but as contributors to systems that work better for everyone inside them.

Current county partnerships include the Contra Costa Health Department, the County Administrator's Office, Employment and Human Services, the Department of Conservation and Development, Behavioral Health, Probation, and the Housing Authority of Contra Costa County.
