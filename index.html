The partnership

Two organizations.
One integrated approach.

Our partnership is built on a simple premise: the people with firsthand experience of public systems hold essential knowledge for improving them. We structure every engagement to bring that knowledge into the room — alongside the agency staff and partners already doing the work.

Organization one
Dalbert Design

A lived experience–led firm specializing in homelessness and housing response systems. We surface the gaps that administrative data misses and translate community knowledge into institutional action.

About Dalbert Design →
Organization two
CivicMakers

A strategic consultancy with 10+ years partnering with governments and nonprofits. We use co-design, community engagement, and systems thinking to shape more just, resilient public services.

What we offer together

A fuller range of capabilities
than either of us alone.

Our partnership gives clients access to what neither organization can offer independently — rigorous human-centered design methods combined with the firsthand knowledge of people who have lived the experiences your programs are designed to address.

01

Lived Experience Integration

Advisor frameworks, compensation structures, and feedback loops that keep community intelligence reaching decision-makers throughout your engagement.

02

Community Engagement

Trust-first strategies, plain language, and co-design sessions that surface genuine insight — not performed consensus — from the communities you serve.

03

Service Design & Evaluation

Participatory research, service blueprints, and actionable evaluation reports tested with the people they're built for.

04

Strategic Planning

Homelessness strategic plans and departmental roadmaps that connect policy intent to community experience — strengthening plan language, improving uptake, and producing better long-term outcomes.

Our approach

Where design meets lived experience.

We bring something most consultancies can't: a team member who has personally experienced the systems your programs are designed to serve — and knows from the inside what works, what's missing, and what would make a real difference.

Step One
Surface what data misses

Participatory research that restores trust, upholds dignity, and uncovers insights that administrative systems routinely obscure.

Lived ExperienceCommunity InputTrust-Building
Step Two
Map the real system

We examine how referral pathways actually work — identifying trust gaps, breakdowns, and design failures that data doesn't capture.

Gap AnalysisSystem MappingDiagnostics
Step Three
Co-design the solution

Working alongside your team and the people you serve to translate learning into plans, tools, and structures with shared ownership.

Co-DesignFacilitationPrototyping
Step Four
Build capacity to continue

Feedback structures that outlast the engagement — so community intelligence keeps reaching decision-makers long after we step back.

Current initiative

The Lived Experience Leadership Institute — LELI

Our signature program trains young adults ages 18–26 from five unincorporated Contra Costa communities in civic intelligence research, AI-directed analysis, and policy engagement. Cohort 1 launches August 2026.
