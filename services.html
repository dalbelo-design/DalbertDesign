What we offer

A fuller range of services
than either of us alone.

Our partnership gives clients access to capabilities neither organization offers independently — combining rigorous human-centered design methods with the firsthand knowledge of people who have lived the experiences your programs are designed to address.

Six service areas

Everything your initiative needs
in one partnership.

Lived Experience Integration

Service Design & Evaluation

Service blueprintsEvaluation reportsParticipatory research

Strategic & Implementation Planning

Homelessness strategic plans and departmental roadmaps that connect policy intent to on-the-ground reality. Lived experience analysis adds the community perspective that strengthens plan language, improves uptake, and produces better long-term outcomes.

Strategic plansImplementation roadmapsOperational protocols

Gap Analysis & System Diagnostics

Who we work with

Built for those doing the hard work.

We partner with the institutions and agencies doing serious work to serve their communities — and who recognize that lived experience makes that work more effective.

County Departments

Health, planning, behavioral health, probation, and housing agencies at the county level.

Nonprofits & CBOs

Community-based organizations working directly with vulnerable populations on housing, workforce, and health.

Government Agencies

State and municipal agencies implementing programs that require meaningful community participation.

Regional Housing Systems

Continuum of care networks, housing authorities, and regional homelessness response systems.

Workforce & Equity Initiatives

Programs connecting underserved populations to economic opportunity through workforce development.

Planning Consultants

Implementation teams that need lived experience expertise to strengthen their own engagements.
